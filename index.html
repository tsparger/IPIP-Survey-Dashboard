Completion rule: a department is counted "completed" when it has at least one submission where the Confirmation question is answered Yes. For the Department Survey that means either InfoCorrect or InfoModified equals 'Yes'; for the Pharmacy Survey it means PharmacyDispenseValidated equals 'Yes'. The same Region + Location + Department combination is counted once regardless of how many times it has been submitted.
